# START HERE - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Mechanic Shop Invoicing System!

This guide is for non-technical users. Everything is designed to be simple and straightforward.

## Installation (First Time Only)

1. **Download** the installer for your computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
2. **Install** by following the on-screen instructions
3. **Launch** the application once
4. **Done!** A desktop shortcut is automatically created

## Using the Desktop Shortcut

After installation, you'll see a **"Mechanic Shop Invoicing"** icon on your Desktop.

**To use the application**: Simply **double-click this icon** to launch it anytime!

That's it! No complicated steps needed.

## First Time Setup (5 minutes)

When you first open the application:

1. Click **Business Settings** in the left sidebar
2. Enter your shop information:
   - Shop name
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Email
   - Add your logo (optional)
3. Click **Save Settings**

## Adding Products (5-10 minutes)

1. Click **Inventory** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Product** button
3. Enter:
   - Product name (e.g., "Oil Filter")
   - Cost price (what you paid for it)
   - Selling price (what you charge customers)
   - Quantity in stock
4. Click **Add Product**
5. Repeat for each product

## Creating Your First Invoice

1. Click **Create Invoice** in the sidebar
2. Fill in:
   - Customer name (optional)
   - Customer phone (optional)
   - Customer email (optional)
3. Click **Add Product** and select from your inventory
4. OR click **Add Labor** to add service work
5. Set the price for labor
6. Review the **Invoice Preview** on the right
7. Click **Create Invoice**

That's it! Invoice is saved and PDF is created automatically.

## Managing Invoices

Click **Invoice History** to:
- See all your invoices
- View invoice details
- Download PDFs
- Mark as paid
- Delete if needed

## Viewing Business Performance

Click **Revenue Tracking** to see:
- Monthly revenue (how much money came in)
- Monthly costs (how much you spent)
- Profit (revenue minus costs)
- Charts showing your business trends

## Key Features

✓ **All data is saved locally** - Everything stays on YOUR computer
✓ **No cloud needed** - Works offline
✓ **Professional invoices** - Automatically formatted and printable
✓ **Philippine Pesos** - All amounts shown in ₱
✓ **Easy backup** - All data in one secure location

## Where Is My Data Stored?

Your data is safely stored on your computer:

**Windows**: `C:\Users\[YourName]\AppData\Local\mechanic-shop-invoicing\`

**Mac**: `~/Library/Application Support/mechanic-shop-invoicing/`

**Linux**: `~/.local/share/mechanic-shop-invoicing/`
